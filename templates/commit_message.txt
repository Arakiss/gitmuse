Given the following git diff, generate a commit message:
{diff}

The commit message should follow these guidelines:
1. Start with a concise summary (max 50 characters)
2. Leave a blank line after the summary
3. Provide more details in the body (if necessary)
4. Use the imperative mood in the subject line
5. Do not end the subject line with a period
6. Wrap the body at 72 characters
7. Use the body to explain what and why vs. how
